ABOUT US

This post is edited by Martins English School

The Agnes Martins Education Society,Sangli was founded in the year 1978 by Mrs. Clara George Martin,an educationalist , who was the founder President of the Society and was Principal of Martins English School,Sangli.
Our school is Government Recognised.
It is a Roman Catholic Christian School and registered with the Asst Charity Commissioner Sangli. English Being the Mother Tongue language of the Martin's , teaching and spoken English with correct pronounciation came naturally as an extension of service to the People of Sangli.

The school consists of Grades from
Kindergarten : Nursery , LKG , UKG / HKG .
Primary : 1st to 7th
Secondary : 8th to 10th

Our School is Co-educational. and our academic session starts from JUNE every year.
